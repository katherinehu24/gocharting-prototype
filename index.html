Tool 01 — Footprint

Footprint & Cluster Charts

A chart that shows the volume traded at each price level inside every candle, broken down by buys and sells.

Read aggression and absorption candle-by-candle, not just the close. Bid×Ask, Delta, Volume, and Imbalance modes — across stocks, futures, forex, and crypto. Color heat scales with traded size; imbalances flag automatically inside each candle.

Tool 02 — DOM

Depth of Market

A live order book showing pending buy and sell orders queued at each price level.

A real, real-time DOM ladder. For scalpers, futures day traders, and anyone reading absorption at the inside, the book is non-negotiable. Click a row to send an order; modify or cancel from the same row.

Tool 03 — Profile

Volume Profile + Market Profile (TPO)

A horizontal histogram of volume traded at each price level — and TPO, the time-based auction structure of the same session.

Volume profile for raw transacted volume; Market Profile (TPO) for the auction structure of the day. Anchor either to a session, a visible range, or a custom period. Point of Control, value area, and single prints are rendered out of the box.

Tool 04 — Delta

Delta & CVD Analysis

Delta — aggressive buying minus aggressive selling per bar. CVD — that delta accumulated across time.

Delta tells you where buyers or sellers are leaning into the tape. CVD tells you whether that pressure is building or exhausting. Both panes share a price axis with the chart above, with no plugin or workaround.
